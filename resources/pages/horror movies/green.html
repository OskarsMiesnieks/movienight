Green Room

2015
1h 35m

Stars: Anton Yelchin, Imogen Poots, Alia Shawkat

A punk rock band is forced to fight for survival after witnessing a murder at a neo-Nazi skinhead bar. A band straying into a secluded part of the Pacific Northwest stumbles onto a horrific act of violence. Because they are the only witnesses, they become the targets of a terrifying gang of skinheads who want to make sure all the evidence is eliminated.
