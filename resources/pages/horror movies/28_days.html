28 Days Later

2002
1h 53m

Stars: Cillian Murphy, Naomie Harris, Christopher Eccleston

A highly contagious, aggression-inducing virus known as the "rage virus" spreads across Great Britain after an infected chimpanzee is released by eco-terrorists from a laboratory in Cambridge. Within moments, the virus infects people, leading to rapid societal collapse. Twenty-eight days after the outbreak, Jim, a bicycle courier who had been in a coma, wakes up in a deserted St Thomas' Hospital in London. He meets survivors Selena and Mark, and together they navigate the chaos.
