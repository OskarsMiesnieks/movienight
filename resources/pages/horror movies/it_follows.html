It Follows

2014
1h 40m

Stars: Maika Monroe, Keir Gilchrist, Olivia Luccardi

A young woman is followed by an unknown supernatural force after a sexual encounter. For 19-year-old Jay, autumn should be about school, boys, and weekends out at the lake. But after a seemingly-innocent sexual encounter, she is plagued by strange visions and the inescapable sense that someone, or something, is following her. Faced with this burden, Jay and her friends must find a way to escape the horrors that seem to be only a few steps behind.
