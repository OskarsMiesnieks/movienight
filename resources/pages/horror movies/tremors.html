Tremors

1990
1h 36m

Stars: Kevin Bacon, Fred Ward, Finn Carter

Natives of a small isolated town defend themselves against strange underground creatures which are killing them one by one. A small town gradually becomes aware of a strange creature which picks off people one by one. But what is this creature, and where is it? At the same time, a seismologist is working in the area, she detects _tremors_. The creature lives underground, and can 'pop up' without warning. Trapped in their town, the town-folk have no escape.
